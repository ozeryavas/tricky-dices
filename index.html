Tricky Dices

Score:

Simple Rules

  • The aim of the game is to score more points.
  • If the dice roll is 1-1, the sum of the dice is multiplied by 2. (4p)
  • If the dice roll is 2-2, the sum of the dice is multiplied by 3. (12p)
  • If the dice roll is 3-3, the sum of the dice is multiplied by 4. (24p)
  • If the dice roll is 4-4, the sum of the dice is multiplied by 5. (40p)
  • If the dice roll is 5-5, the sum of the dice is multiplied by 6. (60p)
  • If the dice roll is 6-6, the sum of the dice is multiplied by 7. (84p)
